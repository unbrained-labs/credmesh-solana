# 10 — Swiss Institutional

## Thesis

Credit, in this world, is a sovereign-grade institutional product. CredMesh dresses as a central bank dresses itself: the canonical issuer of a thing the rest of the world has to use. Not a startup. Not a "platform." A registry. A bulletin. A statistical annex.

This concept rejects the vocabulary of consumer fintech. No cards, no chips, no glow. Liquidity does not "flow" — it is *posted*. An advance is not "drawn" — it is *issued, recorded, footnoted*. Reputation is a digit on a ledger, not a badge.

The page should feel like a Bundesbank monthly report pinned at a typography conference. First impression *seriousness*, second *legibility*, third — only third — that this is software. The promise is institutional gravity: machines borrow from CredMesh because the humans who run them want a counterparty that looks older than it is.

## Design rules

1. **Helvetica Neue only.** 95 Black for masthead, 75 Bold for callouts, 55 Roman for body, 45 Light for footnotes. No second typeface. Inter substitutes when Helvetica isn't licensed.
2. **One accent: sovereign red `#C8102E`.** At most 2× per screen, only on a numeric delta or single rule. Else `#0A0A0A` ink on `#F4F2EE` paper, `#6B6B6B` for secondary type.
3. **No rounded corners. No shadows. No gradients. No glassmorphism. No icons.** Right angles only. Borders are 1px hairlines. Use a rule, not a card.
4. **Tabular figures everywhere a number appears.** Right-aligned. Decimal points stack vertically across rows.
5. **Twelve-column grid, 24px gutters, hard outer margin.** Whitespace is structural — a 4-column gap is a paragraph break.
6. **Tables dominate.** When a chart and table show the same data, ship the table. Charts: 1px stroke, no fill, no legend frame, captioned beneath in 10pt italic.
7. **Footnote every number** with numbered superscript ¹ ² ³ pointing to a source line. This is what separates a bulletin from a dashboard.
8. **Page number, issue number, date stamp top-right of every screen.** As if it could be torn out and filed.
9. **The wordmark is the logo.** "CREDMESH" in Helvetica 95 Black, all caps, optically tightened, with one solid `#C8102E` square as registered-issuer mark. Never lockup with a tagline.
10. **No motion. No hover beyond underline.** Documents don't animate.
