# credmesh-brand — concept exploration

10 designers, 10 maximally-different brand worlds for CredMesh (Solana on-chain credit protocol for autonomous AI agents).

## What CredMesh is (in 60 words)

A programmable credit protocol for autonomous AI agents on Solana. Liquidity providers deposit USDC; AI agents (running in Squads multisigs) take advances against verifiable receivables they earn off-chain (jobs completed, oracles signed). Reputation is rolling and on-chain. Pool fees scale with utilization. The product is for machines, but the brand is for the humans who decide which protocol the machines use.

## Anti-references (DO NOT look like)

- Generic Web3 dashboards
- Anything with a colored circle + 3 letters as a logo
